<p><strong>The Airbag Inflators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic Inflators</li><li>Stored Gas Inflators</li><li>Hybrid Inflators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airbag inflators market features three main types: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to rapidly produce gas, leading to quick deployment in accidents. Stored gas inflators rely on compressed gas stored in a canister, providing a consistent inflation method. Hybrid inflators combine both techniques to enhance performance and reliability, offering rapid deployment while utilizing stored gas for sustained inflation, thus optimizing safety in automotive applications.</p></p>
