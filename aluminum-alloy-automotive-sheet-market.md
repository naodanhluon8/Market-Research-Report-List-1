<p><p>The Aluminum Alloy Automotive Sheet market consists of three main types: cast aluminum, rolled aluminum, and extruded aluminum. Cast aluminum is created by pouring molten metal into molds, ideal for complex shapes. Rolled aluminum is produced by passing aluminum sheets through rollers, resulting in thin, flat sheets used for body panels. Extruded aluminum is formed by pushing aluminum through a die, allowing for various profiles and structural components. Each type offers unique properties suited for automotive applications, enhancing performance and reducing weight.</p></p>
