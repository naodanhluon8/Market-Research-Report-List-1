<p><p>The automotive thermoplastic elastomer market is categorized into several types. Styrene Block Copolymers (SBS, SEBS) offer excellent flexibility and durability, making them ideal for weatherstripping and seals. Thermoplastic Poliolefin (TPO) is known for its lightweight and chemical resistance, commonly used in interior and exterior applications. Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) provides superior abrasion resistance and elasticity, suitable for protective coatings and parts. Other elastomers may include specialty blends and materials tailored for specific automotive requirements, enhancing performance and versatility.</p></p>
