<p><strong>The Brake Wear Indicator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrical Indicator</li><li>Audible Indicator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake wear indicator market is primarily divided into two types: electrical indicators and audible indicators. Electrical indicators use sensor technology to monitor brake pad wear, providing visual alerts to drivers through dashboard lights when maintenance is needed. In contrast, audible indicators utilize sound to signal wear, often producing a warning noise as the brake pads reach their wear limit. Both types aim to enhance vehicle safety by ensuring timely brake maintenance and preventing potential brake failure.</p></p>
