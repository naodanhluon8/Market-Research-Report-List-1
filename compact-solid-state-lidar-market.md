<p><strong>The Compact Solid state LIDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phased Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compact Solid State LIDAR market comprises three main types: MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) Based Scanning, Phased Array, and Non-Scanning Flash. MEMS-based LIDAR employs miniature mirrors to rapidly scan environments, offering compact designs suited for automotive applications. Phased Array LIDAR uses an array of sensors to emit and detect light simultaneously, facilitating instant data acquisition without moving parts. Non-Scanning Flash LIDAR emits a broad light pulse capturing 3D images in real-time, ideal for high-speed applications and dense environments.</p></p>
