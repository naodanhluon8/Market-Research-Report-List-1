<p><p>Dichroic filters are optical devices that selectively transmit light of specific wavelengths while reflecting others. The market for these filters is categorized into single-phase and three-phase filters. Single-phase filters are designed for specific applications where only one type of wavelength needs to be transmitted, often used in simple lighting systems. In contrast, three-phase filters are utilized in more complex scenarios, allowing for the separation of three distinct wavelength bands, commonly used in advanced imaging and illumination systems in various industries.</p></p>
