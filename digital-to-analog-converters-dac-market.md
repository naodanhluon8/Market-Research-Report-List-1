<p><p>Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs) are crucial for converting digital signals into analog form. The market can be segmented into several types: </p><p>- **R-2R** DACs use a resistor ladder network for binary input conversion. </p><p>- **String** DACs employ a series of resistors for precise voltage output. </p><p>- **Current Source and Sink** DACs manage current flow to produce analog signals efficiently. </p><p>- **Others** may include DACs based on sigma-delta modulation or integrated circuits, catering to diverse applications in audio, video, and signal processing.</p></p>
