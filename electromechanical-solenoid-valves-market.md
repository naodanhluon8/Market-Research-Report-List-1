<p><p>The electromechanical solenoid valves market comprises various types catering to diverse applications. Direct-acting solenoid valves operate directly under electrical control, suitable for low-flow conditions. Sub-step direct-acting solenoid valves provide enhanced precision and control by enabling intermediate positions. Pilot solenoid valves, on the other hand, utilize a smaller pilot valve to control a larger main valve, allowing for efficient operation in high-flow systems. Each type serves unique functionality in automation, fluid control, and process management across industries.</p></p>
