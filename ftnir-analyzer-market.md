<p><p>The FTNIR (Fourier Transform Near-Infrared) Analyzer market is divided into two main types: online measurement and offline measurement. Online measurement refers to real-time analysis conducted during production processes, allowing for immediate adjustments and quality control. In contrast, offline measurement involves analyzing samples in a laboratory setting after production, which can provide in-depth insights but may delay results. Both types are crucial for industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing, ensuring product quality and compliance with standards.</p></p>
