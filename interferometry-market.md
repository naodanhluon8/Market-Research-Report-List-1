<p><ul><li>Laser Fizeau Interferometers</li><li>Mach-Zehnder Interferometer</li><li>Michelson Interferometer</li><li>Fabry-Perot Interferometer</li><li>Sagnac Interferometer</li><li>Twyman-Green Laser Interferometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interferometry market encompasses various types of interferometers, each serving distinct applications. Laser Fizeau interferometers measure surface flatness and optical quality. Mach-Zehnder interferometers analyze phase shifts in light for precise measurements. Michelson interferometers are used for distance and wavelength measurements. Fabry-Perot interferometers enhance spectral resolution. Sagnac interferometers exploit rotational motion for gyroscope applications. Twyman-Green laser interferometers evaluate optical components' flatness and coatings. Together, these technologies support industries like metrology, telecommunications, and aerospace, driving advancements in precision measurement and testing.</p></p>
