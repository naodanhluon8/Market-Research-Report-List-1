<p><strong>The Monolithic Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monolithic photonic integrated circuit market is categorized into passive and active segments. Passive photonic integrated circuits do not require external power and are used for functions like signal routing and filtering, utilizing components like waveguides and optical couplers. In contrast, active photonic integrated circuits incorporate components that require power to operate, such as lasers and modulators, enabling functionalities like signal generation and amplification. Both types play crucial roles in communication, sensing, and data processing applications.</p></p>
