<p><p>The natural drinking mineral water market consists of several types based on carbonation levels and processing. Natural mineral water with gas retains its natural carbonation, offering a refreshing effervescence. Aerated natural mineral water, on the other hand, undergoes additional carbonation, enhancing its fizz. Natural mineral water without gas is still rich in minerals but is flat, catering to those preferring still water. Degassed natural mineral water is created by removing carbon dioxide, providing a smooth, non-fizzy option while preserving mineral content.</p></p>
