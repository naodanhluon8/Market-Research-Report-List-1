<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generators</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen generation market comprises two primary types: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) nitrogen generators and membrane nitrogen generators. PSA systems utilize adsorption technology to separate nitrogen from other gases under pressure, offering high purity and efficiency, making them ideal for industrial applications. Membrane nitrogen generators, on the other hand, employ selective permeation through membranes to separate nitrogen from compressed air, providing a continuous flow at lower capital costs. Both technologies cater to varied industrial needs based on application requirements and operational scale.</p></p>
