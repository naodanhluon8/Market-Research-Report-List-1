<p><p>The ophthalmic operating tables market features three main types: electric, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic operating tables. Electric operating tables offer precise positioning and ease of adjustment with motorized controls, enhancing surgical efficiency. Hydraulic tables utilize fluid pressure for adjustments, providing stability and support for various procedures. Electro-hydraulic tables combine both hydraulic and electric functionalities, offering versatile positioning options and improved ergonomics for surgical teams. These differing types cater to diverse surgical needs and improve overall patient outcomes in ophthalmic procedures.</p></p>
