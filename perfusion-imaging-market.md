<p><strong>The Perfusion Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Computed Tomography (CT Scan)</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)</li><li>Nuclear Medicine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perfusion imaging encompasses various imaging modalities designed to assess blood flow and tissue perfusion. Computed Tomography (CT) scan utilizes X-rays to create cross-sectional images, allowing for rapid assessment of perfusion in various organs. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) employs strong magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images, enabling evaluation of blood flow dynamics in soft tissues. Nuclear medicine relies on radioactive tracers to visualize physiological processes, offering insights into perfusion at the cellular level, crucial for diagnosing various conditions.</p></p>
