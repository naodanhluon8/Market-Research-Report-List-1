<p><ul><li>Chemical</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET bottle recycling market consists of two primary types: chemical and mechanical recycling. Mechanical recycling involves collecting, sorting, and shredding PET bottles into flakes, which are then washed and converted into new products, often limiting the quality of the output to similar applications. In contrast, chemical recycling breaks down PET into its fundamental building blocks through chemical processes, allowing for the production of higher-quality raw materials that can be reused for manufacturing virgin-grade PET products.</p></p>
