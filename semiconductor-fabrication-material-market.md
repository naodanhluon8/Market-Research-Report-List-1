<p><p>The semiconductor fabrication material market includes various types crucial for chip manufacturing. Silicon wafers serve as the foundational substrate for devices. Photomasks are used to pattern layers on the wafers, while photoresists enable selective etching during lithography. Wet chemicals assist in cleaning and etching processes. CMP slurry and pads are essential for chemical-mechanical polishing of layers. Gases supply critical elements for deposition and etching, whereas sputter targets provide materials for thin-film deposition. Photoresist ancillaries support photoresist performance, along with other materials that enhance the fabrication process.</p></p>
