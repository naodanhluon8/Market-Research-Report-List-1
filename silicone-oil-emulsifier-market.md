<p><ul><li>Non-Ionic Emulsifier</li><li>Anionic Emulsifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone oil emulsifier market primarily includes non-ionic and anionic emulsifiers. Non-ionic emulsifiers utilize a neutral charge, offering stability and versatility across various applications, including cosmetics and personal care products. They effectively stabilize silicone oil in formulations without reacting significantly with other components. Conversely, anionic emulsifiers carry a negative charge, enhancing emulsification properties in products like detergents and industrial applications. These emulsifiers improve the dispersion of silicone oils, ensuring uniformity and enhancing the overall product performance.</p></p>
