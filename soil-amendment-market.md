<p><p>The soil amendment market can be categorized into three main types: powder, granular, and liquid amendments. Powder soil amendments are finely ground materials, easily mixed into soil to enhance nutrient availability and improve texture. Granular soil amendments consist of larger particles, offering controlled release of nutrients and easier application. Liquid soil amendments are solutions that can be directly applied to the soil or foliage, providing fast-acting nutrient delivery and targeted treatments. Each type serves distinct agricultural and horticultural needs.</p></p>
