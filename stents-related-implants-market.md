<p><p>The stents and related implants market encompasses various types of devices used to maintain vessel patency. Coronary stents are utilized to treat blockages in coronary arteries, often resulting from atherosclerosis, enhancing blood flow to the heart. Peripheral stents address similar blockages in peripheral arteries, improving circulation in limbs. Stent-related implants include accessories such as drug-eluting stents, which release medication to prevent restenosis, and bioresorbable stents that dissolve over time, offering temporary support while promoting natural healing.</p></p>
