<p><p>The thermal insulating mortars market is segmented into two main types: inorganic insulating mortars and organic insulating mortars. Inorganic insulating mortars are primarily composed of materials like cement and silica, offering excellent fire resistance and durability, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. On the other hand, organic insulating mortars, which often incorporate polymers and organic compounds, provide enhanced flexibility and better adhesion properties. Both types serve to improve energy efficiency in buildings and reduce heat transfer, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
