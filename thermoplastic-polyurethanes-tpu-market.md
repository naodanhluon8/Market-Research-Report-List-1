The TPU market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Polyester-based TPU** offers superior strength and heat resistance, making it ideal for automotive and industrial applications. </p><p>2. **Polyether-based TPU** is known for its excellent flexibility and hydrolysis resistance, suitable for medical and textile uses. </p><p>3. **Polycaprolactone-based TPU** provides unique biodegradability and is primarily used in specialty applications like flexible packaging and medical devices.</p></p>
